# THE HILL — Series Trailer (Season One v2)

**Format:** 2:15 theatrical / streaming main trailer  
**Tone:** Southern gothic · dread before spectacle · oral history vs. courthouse silence  
**Music:** Sparse. Insects, wind in ridge timber, single bow or banjo phrase — no heroic swell until final title card.  
**Narration:** Optional dual frame — **1942 porch** (old voice) under **1864** (no omniscient historian). No "based on a true story" card. Use evidence-tier discipline in supers only where needed.

---

## TRAILER SCRIPT

### 0:00 — BLACK

**SFX:** One chicken wing settling in iron skillet. Oil hiss. Then silence.

**SUPER (small, white):** Johnson County, Arkansas · 1864

---

### 0:04 — EXT. HORNER HOMESTEAD — DUSK

A yard too quiet. A ridge above the house. Children posted where children should not be posted.

**CHILD (O.S., distant):** Here they come, Grandpap—

Cut off. No gunshot yet.

---

### 0:11 — INT. HORNER HOUSE — SAME

Fire low. Faces around a table. Chicken on a plate nobody has touched.

**VOICE (1942, old man on porch — not on screen yet):**  
Whatever you do… don't go up to the house in the daytime.

**YOUNG MAN (1864, low):**  
The food's in the house.

Hold on the table. **This is the last ordinary meal.**

---

### 0:22 — EXT. POTATO PATCH — DAY

SPENCER HORNER walks out with his hands up. Open ground. August heat.

**VOICE (1942):**  
Grandfather went out into the potato patch and said, *I surrender!*

Riders at the tree line. Neighbors' faces half-known.

**SFX:** One shot. Not a battle. A field.

---

### 0:32 — SAME — QUICK CUTS (no glamour)

William running. A pistol butt. Rows stomped flat. Fence down. Women dragging weight toward the ridge — not toward the house.

**SUPER:** Twenty riders. Parties known.

---

### 0:40 — EXT. CAVE OVERHANG — NIGHT

Water drip. Breath fighting through a swollen body. Cloth. Silence.

**WOMAN (whisper):**  
We already are.

**SFX:** Chisel on stone (under, from another time).

---

### 0:48 — EXT. CEMETERY — DAY

Fresh cut stone. Partial inscription we hear before we read:

**VOICE (stone carver, O.S.):**  
…killed August the tenth… by parties known to his family…

Camera finds the misspelled surname. **HOMER.** It stays.

---

### 0:56 — INT. SHERIFF'S OFFICE — DAY

Ledger open. Pen dry. Door empty.

**VOICE (1864, flat):**  
The law never came.

A portrait on the wall: **JOHN FRY HILL** — sheriff, colonel, same county.

---

### 1:04 — INT. MUSTER ROOM / BARN — NIGHT

Names on paper. Faces on paper. A finger counting.

**HORNER (O.S.):**  
Twenty men in my father's field.

**MIDDLETON (O.S.):**  
How many names do we have?

**HORNER:**  
Not enough.

---

### 1:12 — MONTAGE — THE SQUAD (no slow-mo hero shots)

Dr. JOHN TURNER HORNER checks a pistol like a man checking a instrument.

**WASH MIDDLETON** — burly, quiet — saddles a horse.

**FATE ARNOLD** — small, iron — watches the tree line.

**JESS WILSON** holds reins. Looks like he wouldn't fight a chicken. Does anyway.

**HORNER:**  
Then we learn the rest.

Horses into timber.

---

### 1:24 — EXT. CORNFIELD — LATE DAY

Stubble. Smoke low. Men between rows. Each man picks his man.

Horner raises a pistol. Hand will not obey.

**SFX:** Shot wide. Pistol thrown. A rider breaks for the ridge and **does not fall.**

**ARNOLD:**  
You let him go.

**HORNER:**  
I didn't let him go.

---

### 1:36 — EXT. FIELD EDGE — DUSK

Blood on cuffs. No badges. No courthouse.

**HORNER:**  
Twenty in the field. One in the rows.

Beat.

**HORNER:**  
Nineteen.

**MIDDLETON:**  
Then we're not done.

**HORNER:**  
No. We're started.

---

### 1:48 — INT. PORCH — 1942 (FRAME DEVICE)

Old PROF. JOHN TURNER HORNER. Summer night. A child on the steps.

**OLD MAN:**  
Dad had a squad of men… desperate men… to go back into the hills and clean out that bunch.

Child listening.

**OLD MAN:**  
They killed seventeen out of the twenty bushwhackers.

**CHILD:**  
What happened to the other three?

The old man looks past the porch light, into dark trees.

**OLD MAN:**  
That's why the hills are still waiting.

---

### 1:58 — EXT. POTATO FIELD — PRESENT DAY (TAG)

Same ground. GPS pin in phone screen. Hand does **not** tap Publish. Hand puts phone away.

Wind in the rows. Ridge above. No score.

**VOICE (present, quiet — Daniel/Bret):**  
The county never wrote it down.

Beat.

**VOICE:**  
So somebody had to go back into the hills.

---

### 2:08 — BLACK

**SFX:** One horse exhale. Then nothing.

**TITLE CARD:** **THE HILL**

**SUBTITLE:** A Southern Gothic Family Series

**TAGLINE (below title):**  
*Before the violence, there was dinner.*

Hold 2 seconds.

**FINAL SUPER (small):**  
*Some stories are not inherited finished. They are inherited waiting.*
