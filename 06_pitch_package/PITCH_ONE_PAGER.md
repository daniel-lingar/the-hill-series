# THE HILL — Pitch One-Pager

## Hook
The roster was not background. It was the map.

## What It Is
A Civil War Ozarks family-history limited series where a modern descendant follows land, cemeteries, oral tradition, and military records into an unresolved 1864 family killing.

## What It Is Not
It is not a revenge fantasy. It is not a clean mystery. It is not ancestor worship.

## Why It Matters
Most families do not inherit finished stories. They inherit fragments, warnings, shame, silence, names, and places. This series asks what responsibility looks like when proof, memory, and pain do not line up cleanly.

## Final Line
This is what really happened — as close as I can honestly stand.
