# Source Discipline

Do not flatten the story into revenge. Do not flatten uncertainty into proof.

Use these lines as operating law:

- **Heard is not nothing. Heard is not proof.**
- **Likely is a word with brakes.**
- **Not proving is not the same as not knowing.**
- **Not knowing is not permission to pretend proof.**
- **Descendants are not defendants.**
- **Account, not avenge.**
