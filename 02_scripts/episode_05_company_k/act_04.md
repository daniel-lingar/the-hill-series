# Episode 5 — Company K
## Act 4 — The Doctor Comes In

**Status:** GAP CLOSED — FIRST FULL SCRIPT PASS

**Evidence Lane:** Dr. John Turner Horner’s exact movements are dramatized from documented family/military context and the claim that he organized Company K. The emotional confrontation is dramatized.

---

### SCENE 1 — EXT. UNION CAMP ROAD — LATE AFTERNOON

A rider comes in without ceremony.

Not heroic. Not clean. Horse spent. Coat dusted. Medical bag tied hard to the saddle.

DR. JOHN TURNER HORNER dismounts like a man whose body has not received permission to be tired.

A picket raises his rifle.

PICKET
Name and business.

JOHN TURNER
Dr. John Turner Horner. Johnson County. I am looking for refugees from the Horner place.

The picket lowers the rifle just a little.

PICKET
Command tent first.

JOHN TURNER
Family first.

PICKET
Orders.

John Turner steps closer.

JOHN TURNER
Son.

The picket does not understand.

JOHN TURNER (CONT'D)
I am asking as a son before I answer as anything else.

The sergeant approaches from behind.

SERGEANT
Let him through.

---

### SCENE 2 — EXT. WAGON SHADE — CONTINUOUS

Mary Ann sees him before anyone speaks.

For a second she looks relieved.

Then she remembers what he does not know.

John Turner slows as he approaches. Permelia rises. John Samuel stands behind her.

JOHN TURNER
Ma.

Permelia’s face holds until he says it. Then it changes, but only at the edges.

PERMELIA
John.

He looks from face to face.

JOHN TURNER
Where is Pa?

No one answers.

His eyes go to Mary Ann.

JOHN TURNER (CONT'D)
William?

Mary Ann’s voice is flat because anything else would break loose.

MARY ANN
Your father was shot at the house. William was left for dead. We found him breathing. We carried him to the cave.

John Turner’s hands close.

MARY ANN (CONT'D)
He lived days.

John Turner looks toward the hills as if he could still ride there fast enough.

JOHN TURNER
Where is he now?

PERMELIA
Dead.

The word lands without cushion.

John Turner takes it like a blow he asked for.

JOHN TURNER
I was escorting families.

MARY ANN
So were we.

He looks at her.

MARY ANN (CONT'D)
Only we were escorting one man between breath and death.

Permelia turns sharply.

PERMELIA
Mary Ann.

MARY ANN
No. Let him hear it. Let him hear something before the men in that tent make it into columns.

John Turner’s eyes fill, but he does not spend the tears.

JOHN TURNER
Tell me all of it.

MARY ANN
There is no all of it.

---

### SCENE 3 — INT. COMMAND TENT — EVENING

John Turner stands over the map.

The officer watches him carefully. The sergeant stands ready to intervene if grief turns into command.

John Turner reads the slips.

SPENCER HORNER.

WILLIAM RILEY HORNER.

FRANCIS MARION ACORD.

PLEASANT HORNER — CONFIRM.

WILLIAM GORDON ACORD — SECONDARY / VERIFY.

HEARD: MIDDLETON. ARNOLD. WILSON.

He stops at Wilson.

JOHN TURNER
Jess Wilson borrowed an auger.

OFFICER
From whom did you hear that?

JOHN TURNER
Mary Ann.

OFFICER
Did she see him with the men?

JOHN TURNER
No.

OFFICER
Then it stays as connected circumstance, not proof.

John Turner turns on him.

JOHN TURNER
You think I need a lesson in proof while my father is in the ground and my brother died in stone?

OFFICER
I think you need one most now.

The tent goes still.

OFFICER (CONT'D)
Because if you ride on grief and call it evidence, every man you hate becomes guilty enough.

John Turner steps close.

JOHN TURNER
Careful.

OFFICER
That is exactly the word.

The officer points to the columns.

OFFICER (CONT'D)
Careful is the only thing keeping this from becoming another raid with a better excuse.

John Turner looks back at the map. Breath hard. Hands shaking.

Then he takes a pencil.

On a blank sheet he writes in large letters:

PROVEN.

SUPPORTED.

HEARD.

QUESTION.

UNKNOWN.

---

### SCENE 4 — EXT. CAMP HOSPITAL AREA — NIGHT

A wounded rider is brought in.

Not one of theirs. Not clearly. His shirt is gray-brown, his accent local, his face pale with blood loss.

A young soldier spits near him.

SOLDIER
Let him bleed.

John Turner looks at the man. Then at the soldier.

JOHN TURNER
Move.

SOLDIER
Doctor, he might be one of—

JOHN TURNER
Move.

John Turner kneels. Opens his bag. Works fast.

Mary Ann watches from the edge.

The wounded man groans.

WOUNDED MAN
Don’t let him cut me.

JOHN TURNER
Then stop bleeding without help.

The man shuts up.

John Turner presses cloth hard.

The soldier who spat watches, ashamed and angry both.

SOLDIER
How do you do that?

JOHN TURNER
Do what?

SOLDIER
Fix him.

John Turner keeps his hands working.

JOHN TURNER
Because if I only practice medicine on men I already forgive, I am not a doctor. I am just another armed man with cleaner tools.

Mary Ann hears that. It does not absolve him. But it gives her a line she can stand near.

---

### SCENE 5 — INT. COMMAND TENT — LATER NIGHT

John Turner returns to the map with blood on his cuffs.

Not enemy blood. Patient blood.

The officer sees it.

OFFICER
You still want to ride?

JOHN TURNER
Yes.

OFFICER
For what purpose?

John Turner looks at the columns.

JOHN TURNER
To locate. To identify. To bring back what can be brought back.

OFFICER
And if you meet Middleton?

JOHN TURNER
I do not know.

OFFICER
That is the first honest answer you have given me.

John Turner sits.

JOHN TURNER
If I do not ride, I will rot here.

OFFICER
If you ride wrong, other people rot because of you.

John Turner nods. He knows.

He writes names in the proper places:

JESS WILSON — BORROWED AUGER / CONNECTION / NOT PROOF.

WASH MIDDLETON — HEARD / REPRISAL TRADITION PENDING.

FATE ARNOLD — HEARD / CONNECTION UNDOCUMENTED.

OTHERS UNKNOWN.

LOCAL MEN / JOHNSON COUNTY.

SPENCER RECOGNIZED ATTACKERS — FAMILY ACCOUNT.

Then he underlines:

HEARD IS NOT NOTHING. HEARD IS NOT PROOF.

---

### SCENE 6 — EXT. CAMP EDGE — PRE-DAWN

Mary Ann finds John Turner outside, sitting on an overturned crate.

For a moment they are just two people who have lost the same man differently.

MARY ANN
You fixed that rider.

JOHN TURNER
I stopped him dying tonight. That is not fixing.

MARY ANN
No. It ain’t.

She sits beside him.

MARY ANN (CONT'D)
I wanted you to come in angry enough to make the world answer.

JOHN TURNER
I am.

MARY ANN
No. I mean loud. Simple. Useful.

John Turner almost smiles.

JOHN TURNER
Anger has uses.

MARY ANN
So does a knife. Doesn’t mean you put it in a child’s hand.

John Turner looks toward John Samuel sleeping under the wagon.

JOHN TURNER
I do not know how to carry this.

MARY ANN
Then write first.

He looks at her.

MARY ANN (CONT'D)
Write before you ride. Write before you shoot. Write before the names get so hot they burn off the difference between known and wished.

John Turner nods slowly.

The map exists now.

But maps can guide rescue.

Or revenge.

**END ACT FOUR.**
