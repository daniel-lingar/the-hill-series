# THE HILL — Season One Bible

## Format
Eight-episode prestige limited series. Five-act structure per episode.

## Genre
Civil War Ozarks family-history drama with a modern descendant research frame.

## Logline
A modern Arkansas descendant reconstructs an 1864 family killing in the Ozarks and discovers that the real inheritance is not revenge or certainty, but learning how to carry an unresolved story without turning it into a weapon.

## Thesis
The story is not about solving a murder. It is about what a family does when a murder is known, remembered, recorded in fragments, and never cleanly answered.

## Evidence Rule
Every historical claim belongs in one of five lanes: Proven, Supported, Tradition, Question, Unknown.

## Ending Principle
The ending is not closure. The ending is a disciplined public account: as close as I can honestly stand.
