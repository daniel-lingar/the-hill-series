# Evidence Lanes

## Proven
Primary records, inscriptions, official military documents, land records, and directly documented facts.

## Supported
Multiple records or consistent record-plus-context alignment, but not full proof.

## Tradition
Family or local oral memory. Powerful, meaningful, and not nothing — but not treated as proof.

## Question
A lead that matters but is not answerable yet.

## Unknown
A gap that must remain named instead of filled with imagination.

Core line: **Heard is not nothing. Heard is not proof.**
